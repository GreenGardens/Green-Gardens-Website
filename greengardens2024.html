GREEN GARDENS / SOFT-ROCK INDIE COLLECTIVE

Leeds' Green Gardens craft a soft and intricate indie-rock sound, valuing tenderness and extremities in equal measure. Taking inspiration from experimental guitar music from the last two decades and wading with that into the water left disturbed by the likes of Sparklehorse and Big Thief, Green Gardens show a more kind, sympathetic face of indie music.

Green Gardens' debut album 'This Is Not Your Fault' (released via Come Play With Me records in partnership with EMI North) was released to a positive critical reaction, selling out multiple shows on their corresponding UK tour as well as strong radio coverage from BBC 6Music. Notable playlisting for the record includes Spotify's Fresh Finds, Fresh Finds Folk, We are Leeds, The Neu Playlist (DIY), Hot New Bands and Melomania, as well as Apple Music's New in Alternative and New in Rock. Recorded in Greenmount Studios with Leeds heavyweights Jamie Lockhart (Mi Mye, The Cribs) and Rob Slater (Yard Act, Carpet, Crake) the band tracked much of the album live, giving it the carnality and immediacy that was required to carry you through the near-linear path through loss and guilt.

In 2024, Green Gardens have teamed up with Joel Johnston (Far Caspian) to make their sophomore album. In abandoning a familiar process and using the studio as a fifth member, the band continue into a space of real sonic experimentation, while still sitting on the base of honest, and warm songwriting.
